Simply Satisfied App

Simply Satisfied creates a personalized meal plan, for a day, based on daily caloric intake goals, diet, and dietary exclusions. Simply Satisfied was created to help ease the stress and frustration of coming up with meal plans. Simply Satisfied was created with HTML, CSS, JavaScript and jQuery.

Secret Stash App

Secret Stash allows you to safely store all of your recipes in an app, with the ability to add, edit and delete them as you choose. You are also able to create folders to help organize your recipes as if you were using a binder, but more efficient. You can share any recipe with a family or a friend, just by giving them the url. I created Secret Stash to give people a more personal recipe app and a way to save and share their family recipes easier and without the risk of loss. Secret Stash was created with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Enzyme, Jest, NPM, RESTFul API, Node.js, PostgreSQL and Express.
